Location

For most adjectives, it comes after the noun, e.g. color, form, religion, nationality, etc.
But for some, it comes before:
beau, joli, double, jeuve, vieux, petit, grand, gros(big), mauvais(bad),demi,bon,nonveau,seplacent

For some adjectives, un homme pauvre(not rich)-un pauvre homme(with pity); curieuse histoire(strange)-femme curiense(full of questions)
